Synopsis Friends is an iconic series that came out in 1994, it ran for 10 seasons. The show is about the lives of 6 people who happen to be friends and neighbors in the same building. Each episode we learn more about these characters and their lives outside their friend group.
Introduction Friends, tells the story of 6 people, Chandler, a miserable guy who works in data processing, Ross, a divorcee who works as a paleontologist, Phoebe who works as a masseuse and sometimes a musician, Monica, works as a chef, Joey, a struggling actor who ends up in a soap opera, and Rachel, who works as a waitress but dreams to be in the fashion business.
Summary Friends is a wonderful show you can binge. It's binge-worthy due to the types of humor they showcase that you don't see in other shows nowadays. It's also a show that can also help, as some episodes showcase lessons that can help guide you in life ( Just don't look for relationship advice in this show ;) )

Characters

Chandler Muriel Bing
 Chandler Muriel Bing, portrayed by Matthew Perry. Chandler is known for his badluck, and his iconic sarcastic jokes
Ross Geller
 Ross Geller, portrayed by David Schwimmer. Ross is known for his unfortunate relationships, his crush on Rachel, his clever personality and his love and knowledge for dinosaurs
Monica Geller
 Monica Geller, portrayed by Courteney Cox. Monica is known for her strong personality, her competitiveness, her strengths, and her love for orderliness.
Rachel Karen Green
 Rachel Green, portrayed by Jennifer Aniston. Rachel is known for her iconic hairstyle, but she's also known for her independent personality, her character growth, and her love for fashion.
Joseph Francis Tribbiani Jr.
 Joey Tribbiani, portrayed by Matt Leblanc. Joey is known to be a bit of an airhead, but he does have a lot of smart moments. He's also an aspiring actor and LOVES FOOD.
Phoebe Buffay-Hannigan
 Phoebe Buffay, portrayed by Lisa Kudrow. Phoebe is known to have a quirky personality, she can talk to ghosts, suddenly understand foreign languages and also writes her own music on her spare time, she's also known for her iconic one-liners.
